Crab Apple Blossom

In the Victorian era, Crab Apple Blossom was one of the shining stars of by the Crown Perfumery Company. Today, Clive Christian has reimagined this historical archive scent whist remaining true to the Clive Christian traditions of concentration, complexity and a dedication to using the finest ingredients.

Bright and sparkling notes of Apple Blossom and exotic Marine Bergamot fuse vibrantly with a modern heart of sharp Green Rhubarb and fresh Mojito fusion, whilst a rich base of creamy Sandalwood and aquatically aromatic Driftwood defines a refreshing yet sophisticated scent.
